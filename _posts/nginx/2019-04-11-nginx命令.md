#### 启动等
- 校验配置 `/export/servers/nginx/sbin/nginx -t -c /export/servers/nginx/conf/nginx.conf`

- 重启 `/export/servers/nginx/sbin/nginx -s reload`

- 启动 `/export/servers/nginx/sbin/nginx -c /export/servers/nginx/conf/nginx.conf`
